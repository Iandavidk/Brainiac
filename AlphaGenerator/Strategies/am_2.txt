RSI+Bollinger Bands
Long position:
• Buy signal when RSI <= 30 and price touches the lower bollinger band.
• Stoploss sightly below the reccent low.
• exit trade when RSI touches 40.
Short position:
• Sell signal when RSI >= 70 and price touches upper bollinger band.
• Stoploss slightly above the recent high.
• Exit trade when RSI touches 60.
